Governance & Control

AI that your legal, compliance,
and security teams will approve

Every agent action is auditable, reversible, and human-approved. Controlled intelligence with enterprise-grade guardrails at every layer.

Human-in-the-Loop

Agents propose, humans approve. Every PCC write-back shows a diff preview. No autonomous writes without explicit approval.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action logged — timestamp, user, data accessed, rationale. Three-layer audit: hooks, middleware, DB triggers. SOX-ready from day one.

Role-Based Access

Agents inherit user permissions. Nurse sees her unit. DON sees her facility. Executive sees the enterprise. Same RBAC you already enforce.

  • Kill switch — Disable any agent instantly, per-facility
